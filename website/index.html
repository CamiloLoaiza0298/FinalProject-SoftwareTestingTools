ABOUT US


Welcome to Impresaurios, your number one source for all things 3D printing. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of 3D printers, with a focus on dependability, customer service, and uniqueness.


Founded in 2020 by Juan Camilo Loaiza, Sebastian Jimenez and Juan Manuel Molina, Impresaurios has come a long way from its beginnings in a home office. When they first started out, their passion for eco-friendly 3D printing drove them to start this business.


We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
